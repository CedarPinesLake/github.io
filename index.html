Welcome to Cedar Pines Lake!

We're thrilled you've chosen our cozy lakefront retreat for your stay in Lancaster, South Carolina. This is the perfect spot to unwind steps from the serene shores of Cedar Pines Lake.

Picture starting your mornings with a cup of coffee on the lake-view deck as the sun rises over the water, or casting a line right from the shoreline for some relaxed fishing. We hope you feel right at home.
